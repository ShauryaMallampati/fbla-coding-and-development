How It Works

Simple steps to recover your lost items

1. Report

Found an item? Report it here with a photo and details about where you found it. Our AI helps verify the content.

2. Search

Lost something? Search our database by item type, location, or date. Browse all reported items in one place.

3. Claim

Submit a claim with proof of ownership. Provide details to verify the item is yours.

4. Retrieve

Once approved by administrators, collect your item from the main office with your student ID.

Important Notice

Unclaimed items are donated after 30 days. Check regularly for your lost belongings!
